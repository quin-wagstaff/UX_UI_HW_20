Well Traveled

An app to help you travel safely in a post-COVID world.

Description

Well Traveled was a post-covid travel app I designed that would help those wanting to travel in a post-pandemic world. A new way to organize your travels and meet new friends along the way.

My Role

UX designer (Individual Project)

Problem

Traveling in a post-pandemic world has become even more of a challenge than ever before. Those looking to vacation to another city, state, or country are tasked with the problem of not knowing what the place will be like and what precautions they should take to keep themselves healthy and stress free.

Solution

We look to take this problem and understand what it is that people would like to know about the places they are traveling to. How might we take the stress of traveling post-pandemic and give the people peace of mind knowing what it is to expect and what they need to do in order to stay safe.

  • User Research

    • We began by conducting six user interviews in order to discover how people have changed and adapted their way of planning a vacation in a post-pandemic world. From the research, we found that even the possibility of getting COVID-19 when traveling is still prevalent in the minds of those we interviewed.

  • Interview Questions

    • How often do you travel in a year? What are your biggest concerns regarding covid at this time? What do you do in your day to day life to protect yourself from covid? Are you concerned with travel restrictions being lifted? How do you prepare and plan for an upcoming trip? What is the biggest pain point when it comes to planning your trip?

“I am fully vaccinated myself, so I am not as concerned for myself but I have had a hard time trusting that lifting a mask mandate will protect people that are vulnerable. I would be worried about people that aren’t vaccinated carrying COVID and passing it through travel, which is frustrating when I am trying to be a responsible traveller.”
“I live with younger children and visit my grandparents often, so I have to be very conscientious of how crowded places I’m going to are, making sure I’m masked up, and washing my hands often. I don’t let these worries stop me from traveling, but it is a hindrance when I see others not taking the pandemic very seriously.”
